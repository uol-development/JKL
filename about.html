OUR FOUNDER'S STORY

The Man Behind The Movement

A Life Dedicated To Helping People Live Healthier Lives

For more than two decades, Dr. Jahangir Kabir has inspired countless people to rethink their relationship with health.

Through his research, education, and advocacy of lifestyle-based healthcare, he has helped popularize the idea that meaningful health improvements often begin with simple but consistent lifestyle changes.

His vision led to the creation of JK Lifestyle — an ecosystem designed to help individuals improve their health through education, guidance, and healthier living.
